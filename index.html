  • You **must** provide a valid, active PayPal email address to receive payouts.

  • **Critical Risk Warning:** If the email provided is incorrect or not a valid PayPal account, the payout will fail.
    • TradeDeck will attempt to process the payout **up to 3 times**.
    • If a payout fails 3 consecutive times due to an invalid or incorrect PayPal email provided by the seller, the associated funds will be permanently held by TradeDeck and **will not be paid out**.
    • These funds will be transferred to TradeDeck's operational account to ensure smooth financial flow and cover administrative costs incurred by repeated failed transactions.
    • It is solely the seller's responsibility to ensure the accuracy and validity of their PayPal payout email.
  • **How to ensure a valid PayPal email:**
    • Ensure the email is registered and verified with PayPal.
    • Log into your PayPal account and check the "Email" section in your settings to confirm it's listed and "Confirmed."
    • Do not use temporary or unverified email addresses.
